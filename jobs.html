Best Practices for Maximum Traffic

✅ DO's

  • • Post 2-3 times per week
  • • Rotate different messages to avoid spam flags
  • • Join groups first, read guidelines
  • • Engage naturally with other posts
  • • Add local languages (Amharic, Oromo)
  • • Post during peak hours (9-11am, 6-8pm)

❌ DON'Ts

  • • Don't spam the same message daily
  • • Don't post identical links in quick succession
  • • Avoid posting in groups > 3 times daily
  • • Never use bots or automated tools
  • • Don't ignore group rules/pinned messages
  • • Avoid over-promotional language
