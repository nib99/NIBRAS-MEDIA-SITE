About Nibras Media

Hey, I'm Nibras from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Welcome to Nibras Media — my personal passion project turned into a daily hub for people who love football, chase crypto opportunities, hunt remote jobs, and explore the latest AI tools.

I started this site because I wanted one place to find:

  • Real-time football updates and honest predictions (EPL, Ethiopian Premier League, AFCON…)
  • Legit free crypto airdrops and beginner-friendly exchange guides
  • Remote & local job opportunities that actually pay (especially for Africans)
  • Curated AI tools and prompts that save time and money

Why This Site Exists

In a world full of noise, I believe in cutting through hype with practical, no-BS content. No paid fake reviews. No clickbait. Just useful info from someone living the same reality as many of my readers in Ethiopia and East Africa.
